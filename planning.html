1. Choose Your Destination

Select a destination that fits your budget and interests. Check our destination page for ideas.

2. Set Your Budget

Determine how much you can spend and allocate funds for flights, accommodation, food, and activities.

3. Book Essentials

4. Pack Smart

Budget Travel Tips

Travel Off-Season

Save money by visiting during shoulder seasons when prices are lower.

Use Public Transport

Buses, trains, and metros are often cheaper and more authentic than taxis.

Eat Local

Street food and local markets offer delicious, affordable meals.

Join Tours

Group tours can be cheaper and provide great opportunities to meet other travelers.
